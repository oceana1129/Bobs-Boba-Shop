OUR STORY

We started off as two roommates who were just looking for a nice place to share some drinks and study together. We combined our love of art with our love for sweet drinks, and that starts the very beginnings of ‘Bobs Boba’.

Bobs Boba isn’t just a place where you can share a drink with friends. It is also a place where you can come, grab a nice book, and find yourself immersed in a world of peace and quiet. We want you to come out of our doors feeling a little happier and healthier than before.

No matter what type of tea you like, there is something here for everyone. We hope to bring a refined bubble tea culture into your life.
